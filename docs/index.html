Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

aiese classifies research on artificial intelligence (AI) methods within the software engineering (SE) domain, as well as the more specific model-driven software engineering (MDSE) domain. The classified research papers are taken from a literature review and an analysis of presented SE practices and AI applications.

Introduction back to ToC

ai4se categorises AI applications for SE based on the purpose of applying AI, the target of AI within SE such as requirements engineering or the testing process, the type of AI used, and the level of autonomy achieved by the AI. ai4se is defined in OWL. It is based on the lightweight implementation of the universal foundation ontology (gUFO) and on the computer science ontology (CSO).
